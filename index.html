VR Doctor Training Simulator: LMA Insertion


This VR prototype aims to teach individuals how to insert an Laryngeal Mask Airway. This was a freelance project with Dr. Zachery Tan and Dr. Alfonso Rodriguez Herrera from Kilkenny hospital. I was the lead programmer on the project. It was playtested by the National Ambulance Service and UCD medical students. It was showcased at the 2020 IGBL Conference. It received the runner up award for The Best Technical Achievement at the IMIRT 2019 Game Awards. It received the Best Medical/Education Management Poster award as well as the Best Overall Oral Poster Presentation award at the 2020 Irish Paediatric Association Conference.

For this project my role was lead programmer
